Job Description: Senior Flutter (Co-ordinator)

Job Description:

As a Mobile App Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality mobile applications that deliver exceptional user experiences. You will work closely with our cross-functional teams to create innovative solutions and drive the success of our mobile app projects.

Key Requirements:

  1. A minimum of 3 years of mobile development experience, with a strong focus on Flutter.
  2. Proficiency in Firebase services and Firebase-related development.
  3. Experience with Azure DevOps for project management and CI/CD processes.
  4. Knowledge of PWA development and a demonstrated ability to create progressive web applications.
  5. Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills with a strong focus on mobile app performance and user experience.
  6. Strong teamwork and communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with diverse teams.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Flutter Development: Lead the development of robust, maintainable, and responsive mobile applications using the Flutter framework.
  • Experience: Bring at least 3 years of mobile development expertise with a focus on Flutter.
  • Firebase Integration: Implement Firebase services, including real-time databases, authentication, cloud functions, and more.
  • Azure DevOps: Utilize Azure DevOps for agile project management, CI/CD pipelines, and collaboration.
  • PWA (Progressive Web Apps): Develop Progressive Web Apps and ensure cross-platform compatibility and performance.
  • Bonus Skills: Leverage experience with building apps and software for consumer goods to enhance our product offerings.
  • Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including designers and backend developers, to deliver outstanding user experiences.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
  • Proficiency in JavaScript and its modern framework Flutter.
  • Strong knowledge of mobile app architecture and design principles.
  • Experience with RESTful APIs and third-party libraries.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced and collaborative environment.

If you are a talented Mobile App Developer with a passion for creating exceptional user experiences and want to be part of a dynamic team, we would love to hear from you. Apply today and let's build the future together!
