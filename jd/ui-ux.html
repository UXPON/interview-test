Job Description: UI/UX Designer (Co-ordinator)

Job Description:

Join us as a UI/UX Designer at uxPon! We are looking for a talented designer to create exceptional user experiences.

Key Requirements:

  1. A Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design, Interaction Design, or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
  2. Proven experience as a UI/UX Designer, with a strong portfolio showcasing your design skills and the use of model designer tools.
  3. Proficiency in model design tools such as Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD, or similar software.
  4. Knowledge of user-centered design principles, usability testing, and design thinking methodologies.
  5. Experience in Healthcare Mobile Apps or Related: Previous work with healthcare mobile apps or related experience is preferred.
  6. Strong communication and collaboration skills to work effectively with cross-functional teams.
  7. Attention to detail and the ability to turn complex concepts into elegant and accessible user interfaces.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop intuitive and engaging user interfaces while ensuring a user-centered design approach.
  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and interactive mockups to visualize design concepts and user flows.
  • Design visually stunning and responsive user interfaces for web and mobile applications.
  • Conduct usability testing to gather feedback and iterate on designs for continuous improvement.
  • Utilize model design tools (e.g., Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD) proficiently to create design assets and collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams.
  • Implement a cohesive design language, including color schemes, typography, and icons, to maintain brand consistency.
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure the seamless implementation of designs and address technical constraints.

Qualifications:

If you're a passionate UI/UX Designer with experience in model design tools and a portfolio that demonstrates your design expertise, especially in healthcare mobile apps or related areas, we encourage you to apply.

To apply, please send your resume and portfolio (if available) to hr@uxpon.com.
