Job Description: Senior Mobile APP

Job Description:

As a Mobile App Developer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality mobile applications that deliver exceptional user experiences. You will work closely with our cross-functional teams to create innovative solutions and drive the success of our mobile app projects.

Key Requirements:

  1. Minimum 5 years of experience: You should have a proven track record of at least 5 years in mobile app development.
  2. Strong Knowledge of React Native or Vue Native: Proficiency in React Native or Vue Native is essential, with a deep understanding of the framework's architecture and components.
  3. Animation Expertise: You should possess a strong command of animation techniques and be able to implement fluid and visually appealing animations within mobile applications.
  4. E-commerce App Experience: Experience working on e-commerce applications is highly desirable. Understanding the unique challenges and requirements of e-commerce platforms is a plus.
  5. Deployment Skills: Familiarity with deployment processes and the ability to deploy mobile applications to App Center or similar platforms is a strong advantage.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with the development team to design, develop, and maintain mobile applications.
  • Create user interfaces and experiences that are intuitive and visually appealing.
  • Implement animations and transitions to enhance the user experience.
  • Work closely with product managers and designers to translate concepts into working solutions.
  • Troubleshoot and debug mobile applications to ensure optimal performance.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to drive innovation.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
  • Proficiency in JavaScript and its modern frameworks (React Native, Vue Native).
  • Strong knowledge of mobile app architecture and design principles.
  • Experience with RESTful APIs and third-party libraries.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Strong communication and teamwork skills.
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced and collaborative environment.

If you are a talented Mobile App Developer with a passion for creating exceptional user experiences and want to be part of a dynamic team, we would love to hear from you. Apply today and let's build the future together!
