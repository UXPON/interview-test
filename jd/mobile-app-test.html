Job Description: Senior Mobile APP

Section 1: General Knowledge

  • Can you explain the key differences between native app development and cross-platform development using React Native or Vue Native?
  • How do you optimize the performance of a mobile app, and what tools or techniques do you use for profiling and debugging?
  • What is the significance of responsive design in mobile app development, and how do you ensure your apps are responsive across different devices and screen sizes?

Section 2: Technical Knowledge

  • Could you describe the component lifecycle in React Native or Vue Native, and how do you handle state management within your mobile apps?
  • What are some best practices for implementing animations in mobile apps, and can you provide an example of a complex animation you've created in the past?
  • How do you handle data synchronization and real-time updates in e-commerce mobile applications, especially when dealing with large datasets and multiple users?

Section 3: Project Experience

  • Please share details about a challenging project you've worked on in the past. What obstacles did you face, and how did you overcome them?
  • Have you integrated third-party APIs or SDKs into your mobile apps? If so, can you discuss a specific integration and the challenges you encountered?
  • Can you provide examples of your contributions to the development of e-commerce mobile apps, including any innovative features or improvements you introduced?

Section 4: Practical Skills

  • During this interview, we'd like you to demonstrate your coding skills. Please take a few minutes to code a simple mobile app component that displays a list of items fetched from a mock API. You can choose either React Native or Vue Native for this exercise.
  • Imagine you're tasked with adding a smooth transition effect between two screens in a mobile app. How would you approach this task, and what code would you write to achieve it?
  • Please walk us through your process for deploying a mobile app to App Center (or another preferred platform). What steps are involved, and how do you ensure a successful deployment?
