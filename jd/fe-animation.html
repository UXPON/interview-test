Job Description: Frontend Developer (Animation and Performance Focus)

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including designers and backend developers to implement engaging and seamless user interfaces.
  • Utilize your strong knowledge of animation web techniques to bring creativity and interactivity to our web applications.
  • Develop animations, transitions, and visual effects using cutting-edge technologies, with a particular focus on three.js to create 3D experiences (experience in three.js is a plus).
  • Build responsive websites that deliver a consistent and exceptional user experience across various devices and screen sizes.
  • Optimize website performance by identifying and addressing bottlenecks, minimizing render times, and reducing load times.
  • Employ your expertise in improving DOM performance, optimizing JavaScript code, and implementing lazy loading strategies for efficient resource utilization.
  • Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging technologies to continuously enhance our frontend development processes.

Qualifications:

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Web Development, or related field (or equivalent practical experience).
  • Proven experience as a Frontend Developer with a strong portfolio showcasing your animation and web development skills.
  • Expertise in animation web techniques and a deep understanding of user experience design principles.
  • Proficiency in three.js is a significant plus.
  • Solid grasp of responsive web design and mobile-first development practices.
  • Strong ability to diagnose performance issues, analyze bottlenecks, and implement effective solutions for improved website speed and responsiveness.
  • Experience with optimizing DOM performance and implementing lazy loading techniques.
  • Proficient understanding of web markup, including HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Advanced knowledge of JavaScript programming and frameworks.
  • Familiarity with version control systems, preferably Git.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive attitude towards challenges.
  • Effective communication skills and ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
  • A keen eye for detail and a commitment to delivering high-quality work.

If you're a passionate Frontend Developer who thrives on pushing the boundaries of web animation and performance, and if you're excited to contribute your skills to a dynamic team, we encourage you to apply. Join us in shaping the next generation of immersive and high-performing web experiences.
